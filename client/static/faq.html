FAQ

Usage

How do I register?
Go the main page and fill the form to create an account.

How do I upload Pokémon?
To upload a Pokémon on Porybox, you need:
  • A Porybox account
  • A decrypted save file of your Pokémon game
  • Or the pk6 files of the Pokémon you wish to upload
Please refer to the Extracting Pokémon help page for more information on how to obtain pk6/pk7 files.
When logged in, click on the + sign at the top of the page to upload save or pk6/pk7 files to a box of your choice.

How do I trade a Pokémon to someone?
Porybox does not have a trading interface, but you can use it to facilitate trades negotiated on other platforms.
  1. Show one of your viewable Pokémon to another person and agree to a trade.
  2. Complete the trade in-game, or exchange pk6 files through email or some other method.
  3. Delete the copy of your Pokémon from your account.
When you trade a Pokémon away, always be sure to delete your copy on Porybox. Otherwise, when the other person uploads its pk6 to Poybox, it will show up as a clone.

What do Viewable, Public, and Private mean?
  • visibility Viewable (default): the Pokémon can be viewed (but not downloaded) by other people. Sensitive information, such as its PID and EC, is hidden to the public.
  • public Public: anyone can view or download the Pokémon.
  • lock Private: only you can view and download the Pokémon's data.

What do Listed and Unlisted mean?
  • Listed (default): the box is listed on your user profile, and can be accessed either through that link or by directly visiting the URL.
  • lock_outline Unlisted: the box is not accessible from your user profile, but can be viewed by anyone with the URL.

How do I use formatting in my notes?
See the Markdown guide.

What is read-only mode?
Read-only mode is enabled when Porybox is under maintenance in order to protect your data. When read-only mode is on, you will still be able to view things that have already been uploaded, but you will not be able to upload files or change anything on your account or boxes.
Maintenance usually does not last for more than a day. If Porybox is undergoing maintenance, you can check /r/porybox for more information.

Is my data safe?
Your data is regularly backed up to an third-party server.

Legality

What is considered a clone?
A Pokémon is considered a clone if another Pokémon exists in the Porybox database that shares all the following characteristics:
  • OT
  • TID (Trainer ID)
  • SID (Secret ID)
  • PID (Pokémon ID)
  • EC (Encryption Constant)
  • Evolutionary line
For static PID events, the IVs of the Pokémon must also match in addition to the criteria listed above.

Can I edit Pokémon with Porybox?
Porybox is a Pokémon viewer, not an editor. You will not be able to edit your Pokémon with it.

Community

Where can I get support?
Please read this FAQ and make sure your issue is not already listed. If the FAQ is insufficient, please create a thread on the official support subreddit or ask for help in the IRC channel, #porybox on synirc.net.

Where can I report bugs?
Opening an issue on GitHub is the preferred way to report bugs. If you do not have a GitHub account, bugs can also be reported on reddit or in our IRC channel.

I have an idea, where do I submit it?
Ideas and suggestions can be discussed on the official support subreddit.
