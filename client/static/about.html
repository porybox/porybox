About Porybox

What is Porybox?

Porybox is an online Pokémon viewer. It helps you organize your Pokémon collection online, see advanced information that is usually hidden in game, and share links to individual Pokémon or boxes to show off. You can also make Pokémon publicly available for download, or download Pokémon that have been made public.

Who is behind Porybox?

Porybox was created by the moderators of /r/pokemontrades, a large Pokémon-trading community on reddit that focuses on legitimate, legal and uncloned Pokémon.

Contributing to Porybox

Porybox is open source and we welcome contributions to the project. Check out the wiki on GitHub for information on how to participate!
